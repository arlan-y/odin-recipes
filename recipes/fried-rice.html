Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Optional: Meat / Protein
  • Salt / Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs, season with salt, and fry, but don't cook them too well
  2. If you are using meat, cook it now and set aside
  3. Remove the eggs and place into separate bowl, then add the rice and fry
  4. Add soy sauce to taste
  5. Add eggs and protein
  6. Mix and stir well
  7. Season and serve

Note on Rice

Some people swear by using refrigerated, leftover rice. This is completely fine, but in my experience it doesn't matter too much. You can use freshly cooked rice and your fried rice will still be delicious. However, if you are going to freshly prepare your rice, make sure to use a little less water than normal so that the rice doesn't turn out too wet and sticky.
