Tomato Eggs

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Ketchup
  • Salt / Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs and fry, but don't cook them too well
  2. Remove the eggs and place into separate bowl, then fry the tomatoes
  3. Add ketchup if desired to add flavor and thicken sauce
  4. Add eggs and mix well
  5. Season and serve
